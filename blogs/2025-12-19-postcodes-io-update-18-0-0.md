Postcodes.io 18.0.0 introduces 13 new geographic fields from the ONS Postcode Directory covering healthcare, economic, and environmental data. The update migrates all census-based geographies from 2011 to 2021 Census boundaries, while keeping 2011 data accessible via alias fields for backwards compatibility.
